It's Showtime in a terminal near you! Put on your best colours, resize to 80 columns, and let rip!

Unlike traditional screen-casting, the watcher can copy-paste directly from your termshows (try with the link below).

Installation instructions (`gem install showterm`) have been showtermed for your enjoyment:

Linking

Each termshow gets its own link (which should be very hard to guess). You can add hash-fragments to specify how to play it.

  • http://showterm.io/9c58deae498d39a04e4f3
    — play at normal speed
  • http://showterm.io/9c58deae498d39a04e4f3#fast
    — play at double speed
  • http://showterm.io/9c58deae498d39a04e4f3#stop
    — dont animate, just display the final result

Embedding

You're welcome to embed termshows in your own website. Simply record as usual, and embed an iframe with width=640px. #fast and #stop work as usual.

<iframe src="http://showterm.io/9c58deae498d39a04e4f3" width="640" height="480"></iframe>
