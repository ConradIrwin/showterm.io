It's Showtime in a terminal near you! Put on your best colours,
resize to 80 columns, and let rip!

Unlike traditional screen-casting, the watcher can copy-paste
directly from your terminal (try with the link below).

Installation instructions (`gem install showterm`) have been showtermed for your enjoyment:

Embedding

You're welcome to embed showterms in your own website.
Simply record as usual, and embed an iframe with width=640px (for the lulz)

<iframe src="https://showterm.herokuapp.com/b6803679cb1c9fbcf667cb7cfe8f605e3ce1fe03" width="640" height="480"></iframe>
